Welcome

This is not a tutoring program. It's not an enrichment program. It's not preparation for exams or competitions.

This project is about helping children develop a long-term relationship with mathematical thinking—through curiosity, exploration, and the joy of asking questions.

What We Value

Curiosity over answers. Wondering over certainty. Persistence over speed.

Children should experience mathematics the way mathematicians do—as an ongoing exploration of questions, not a collection of answers. Some questions are valuable even when no answer is immediately available.

Success Looks Like

  • A child thinking about a question all week
  • Noticing patterns nobody mentioned
  • Asking questions nobody can answer immediately
  • Dinner-table mathematical discussions
  • Comfortably saying "I don't know yet"
