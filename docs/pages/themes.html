Mathematical Themes

Possible areas of exploration for mathematical curiosity.

Prime Numbers

Why are some numbers prime? Are there infinitely many? How do primes appear visually?

Geometry

Can shapes tile the plane? How many dimensions exist? What makes certain shapes special?

Infinity

Is there a biggest number? Are all infinities the same size? Can we compare the uncountable?

Number Systems

Why do we count in base ten? Could we use base twelve? Could we invent our own system?

Probability

What does random mean? Can randomness be predicted? When does intuition fail?
