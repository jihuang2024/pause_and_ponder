This Week's Questions

1. Are these numbers prime numbers?

7, 18, 19, 27, 97, 133, 163, 513, 3519871

Hint: Remember, a prime number is only divisible by 1 and itself. Try testing each one.

2. Find a prime number between 200 and 300

Challenge: Can you find more than one? What's interesting about them?

3. How many prime numbers in the entire world?

Think about this: Is there a biggest prime number? Could we ever count all of them?

Share your thinking! These questions don't have easy answers. The fun is in exploring them. What do you notice? What patterns emerge? What new questions come up as you work?
